My two personal favorite city´s are Cefalú and palermo, I´ve been here multiple times and they both suprise me every time. The thing I love about Cefalú is that during the day it´s really quiet and peacefull and the only people you´ll see are tourists and shop owners, and once it gets dark you´ll see the residents go out to have dinner for example. You´ll see entire family´s walking and chatting and it´s just a lovely view. And the reason I love Palermo so much is because it´s alive, and by that i mean that it´s a real city with lots of shops and tourists but also the ambiance is just great everybody is nice to you!
